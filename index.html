LIL' DOZEN ORIGIN


In a community facing economic challenges, a local baker noticed many people struggling to afford basic necessities. Wanting to help, the baker started selling extra boxes of doughnuts, with the idea to give the same amount to local charities. Word spread, and some residents started buying the boxes to support the cause. Over time, the initiative helped provide more food to those in need, but it remained a simple effort without much fanfare, just a way to give back to the community.
