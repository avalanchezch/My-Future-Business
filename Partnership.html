PARTNERSHIP

Bahay Kanlungan

Lil' Dozen's partnership with Valenzuela's Bahay Kanlungan reflects a heartwarming journey of collaboration and shared values. The partnership began as a testament to Lil' Dozen's commitment to community solutions and making a positive impact. Recognizing the importance of supporting initiatives that provide care and love to the elderly and disadvantaged members of society, Lil' Dozen found a natural alignment with Bahay Kanlungan's mission.

The growth of this partnership is rooted in shared principles of compassion and a desire to create meaningful change. Over time, Lil' Dozen actively participated in Bahay Kanlungan's events, contributing not only through financial support but also by engaging in hands-on activities that brought joy to the residents. The genuine care demonstrated by Lil' Dozen resonated with the vision of Bahay Kanlungan, fostering a strong and enduring connection.

As the partnership flourished, Lil' Dozen expanded its involvement, sponsoring specific programs and collaborating on initiatives aimed at enhancing the quality of life for the elderly and persons with disabilities. This collaborative journey is a testament to the belief that businesses can play a pivotal role in creating a ripple effect of happiness and kindness in the community. Lil' Dozen's partnership with Bahay Kanlungan exemplifies the transformative power of businesses that genuinely care about making a difference in people's lives.

Save the Children

Lil' Dozen's partnership with Save the Children epitomizes a shared commitment to building a better world for children. From the Philippines to global initiatives, Save the Children tirelessly endeavors to provide children with a healthy start in life, opportunities to learn, and protection from harm. In times of crises, when children are most vulnerable, Save the Children is at the forefront of immediate response, ensuring that the unique needs of children are met.

The growth of the partnership with Save the Children is rooted in a mutual dedication to transformative impact. Lil' Dozen, driven by the belief that businesses can contribute significantly to positive change, found alignment with Save the Children's mission. The partnership evolved into a powerful collaboration that not only involves financial support but also active engagement in initiatives aimed at delivering lasting results for millions of children.

As Lil' Dozen and Save the Children work together, their shared vision becomes a reality. The partnership ensures that children's voices are heard, their needs addressed, and their lives transformed. Together, they go above and beyond, doing whatever it takes for and with children. This collaboration exemplifies the profound impact that businesses, like Lil' Dozen, can have when partnering with organizations like Save the Children to shape a brighter future for the children we all share.

Philippine Red Cross

The Philippine Red Cross, a prominent humanitarian organization, has been an invaluable partner in Lil' Dozen's mission to make a positive impact in the community. With a shared commitment to addressing and alleviating community problems, the Philippine Red Cross and Lil' Dozen collaborate on various initiatives aimed at making a significant difference in people's lives. Whether it's providing support during disasters, participating in community outreach programs, or contributing to healthcare initiatives, the partnership between Lil' Dozen and the Philippine Red Cross exemplifies the power of businesses and humanitarian organizations working together for the greater good. Through this collaboration, Lil' Dozen not only sweetens the lives of its customers but also actively contributes to creating a safer and more resilient community.

Caritas Manila

Lil' Dozen's collaboration with Caritas Manila represents a commitment to social responsibility and community upliftment. Caritas Manila, originally established as Catholic Charities in 1953, is dedicated to supporting the less fortunate in the community, aligning with Lil' Dozen's mission to make a positive impact. The partnership focuses on total human development and poverty alleviation, working towards the creation of a Christian community with a strong social conscience. Lil' Dozen actively contributes to Caritas Manila's initiatives, bringing assistance to those in need and embodying the values of generosity and compassion in fostering a caring community.

Through this collaboration, Lil' Dozen demonstrates the potential for businesses to play a meaningful role in addressing societal challenges, emphasizing the importance of collective efforts in creating positive change and supporting those facing hardships.
